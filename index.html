How does AI receipt scanning work?

Point your camera at any receipt and our AI technology automatically extracts the merchant name, amount, and date. Make sure the receipt is well-lit and all text is clearly visible for best results.

How do I use voice input?

Tap the microphone button and speak naturally. Say something like "fifteen dollars coffee" or "25 euros lunch" and the app will automatically add it to your expenses.

How do I set my daily spending limit?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Daily Limit, then enter your desired amount. The app will track your progress throughout the day and show visual indicators when you're approaching your limit.

Can I export my expense data?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data to download your expenses in CSV or PDF format for external analysis or record keeping.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. All your expense data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal financial information with third parties.
